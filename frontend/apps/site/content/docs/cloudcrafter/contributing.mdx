---
title: Contributing
description: How to contribute to CloudCrafter
---

We are always looking for contributors to help us improve CloudCrafter. If you are interested in contributing, please check out our 
[GitHub repository](https://github.com/cloudcrafter-oss/CloudCrafter) and see where you can help. If you are unsure on what to do, please do not hesitate to open up a [Discussion](https://github.com/cloudcrafter-oss/CloudCrafter/discussions).

### Pull Requests

If you want to open up a pull request, please target the `main` branch. We will review your PR and give you feedback on it. Once it is approved, we will merge it into the `main` branch.
